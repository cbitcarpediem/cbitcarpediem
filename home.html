GOD OF ALL CAMPUS EVENTS

March 24 - March 25

PRO-SHOWS

Nucleya

25th March, 2017

Nucleya and EDM are close to synonymous in Bollywood - and we're not sure which brought the other into the limelight, but we're glad it happened. Carpe Diem is hosting Nucleya, or Udyan Sagar, on day two. Whether you are an EDM lover or a music aficionado who shuns categories, you can expect it to be, for lack of a stronger word, on fire.

Armaan Malik

24th March, 2017

Few are immune to the charms of Bollywood, and fewer still to some of its best music. Armaan Malik, who gave us Besabriyaan and Sau Asmaan, will be leaving us mesmerised (and a little unwilling to go home) at Carpe Diem. With music reminiscent of summer showers and sepia-toned romances, we have no doubt that you will be yearning for more.

Hema Chandra

24th March, 2017

A soulful voice and the right atmosphere can perform magic and make your day. Hemachandra, of Sa Re Ga Ma Pa fame and with various hits under his belt, is coming to Carpe Diem to provide the former, while we attempt to create the latter. We invite you to let the magic work on you, and have some fun while you're at it.

Sahil

25th March, 2017

Who hasn’t heard of stand up comedy? Everyone loves that funny, goofy person who has a hatke take on everything. Sahil Sharma puts the “pun” in punishment and the “ease” in sleaze, refers to himself as an occasional Time Traveller who has mastered the art of intentional puns.

Sapan Verma

25th March, 2017

Sapan Verma is one of India’s most popular and experienced English stand up comedians,when he isn’t tickling audience’s funny bones at live shows, he writes,directs and even teaches filmmaking to college students.
