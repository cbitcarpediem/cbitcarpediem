CBIT Carpediem brings to you " The Robotic Workshop" : A workshop with a completely different concept, where you learn and then apply the knowledge to solve real time problem statements(Robothon). The workshop will cater to all categories of students from the beginners to the experienced ones. So, don't worry even if you don't know anything related to robotics, all we want in you is the interest and enthusiasm to learn.

The winners of the Robothon get a chance to be a part of the biggest robotic fest in Asia - "The Robofest".

Topics covered : Basics of robotics and arduino uno, basic and advanced robos using the arduino uno including swarm robot and pole climber

Dates : 16th and 17th of March
Venue : Assembly Hall, CBIT
